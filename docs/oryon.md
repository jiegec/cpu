# Qualcomm Oryon

![](./oryon.svg)

Oryon-M in Qualcomm 8 Elite:

1. 4-wide decode instead of 8
2. 4 integer pipelines instead of 6
3. 2 load store pipelines instead of 4
4. 2 fp/simd pipelines instead of 4
